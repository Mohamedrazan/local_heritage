Bab Makkah – Historic Gate of Jeddah

Bab Makkah is one of the most powerful landmarks of Historic Jeddah. Built from solid coral stone, it once protected the city and served as the main gateway for pilgrims and traders traveling toward Makkah. Today, it stands as a symbol of strength, heritage, and Jeddah's deep historical roots.
